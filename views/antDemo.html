Swarm Intelligence: Ants!

A simulation that borrows heavily from swarm intelligence, this demonstration sets out to emulate an ant nest and the ant's search for food.

Clear will empty the current screen and start will restart the game from scratch.

Stats

  • Cohesion: The "glue" force, or what draws the ants toward one another
  • Separation: The repelling force, or what drives the ants away from one another
  • Alignment: The force that keeps the ants headed in the same direction
  • Pheromone: The force that pulls an ant toward a pheromone
